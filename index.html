Webpage Description:

Welcome to my website! Here, you'll find a unique platform designed specifically for actors rehearsing a play inspired by "The Office." This website offers a convenient and interactive way to engage with the script, allowing actors to seamlessly navigate through their characters' lines. Actors can simply touch or click on their character's name within the play. This action will highlight their lines, making it easier to identify and focus on their specific dialogue during rehearsals. This feature allows actors to efficiently practice their lines and perfect their performances.

Script:

"Michael: Alright, team! Today, we're going to do some team-building exercises. Get ready for some fun!

"Jim: Oh, this should be interesting."

"Dwight: I'm ready, Michael! I'm always up for team-building exercises. They make us stronger!"

"Michael: That's the spirit, Dwight! Okay, first up, we're going to do the Trust Fall."

"Jim: Seriously, Michael? We've done this before."

"Michael: Well, this time we're adding a twist. Dwight, you'll be catching Jim. Jim, you'll be catching me. And I'll catch... myself!"

"Jim: You're going to catch yourself?"

"Michael: Yes, Jim! I am my own safety net. It's a metaphor for leadership."

"Dwight: It's a metaphor for idiocy"

"Jim: Alright, Michael. Let's do this."

"Michael: Here we go! Three, two, one... Trust fall!"
